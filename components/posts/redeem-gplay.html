Redeeming Google Play Giftcards

Google Play is the biggest market place for Android applications.


Once you redeem a giftcard that is still active, if it has not been redeemed by any other, the funds will be added to your Google Play balance.

Redeeming on Mobile

To redeem you can follow these simple steps.
1. Open you're Google Play application
2. Click on you're profile at the Top Right Corner



3. Locate the Payments & Subscriptions option and click it.



3. Locate the Redeem Code option and click it.



4. Enter the given code and press Redeem



Redeeming on PC

Redeeming on PC is the easiest way to redeem.


1. Make sure you are signed into the Google Account you want to redeem with on you're Browser
2. Visit Google Redeem
3. Wait a few seconds and a popup will appear asking for your Code
