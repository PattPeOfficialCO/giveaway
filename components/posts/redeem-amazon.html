Redeeming Amazon Giftcards

Amazon is a giant market place, at this place you can buy almost anything you can imagine.


To redeem a giftcard from Amazon, you will need an account on Amazon.com and be signed into that account.


Redeeming on PC

Redeeming on PC is the easiest way to redeem.


1. Make sure you are signed into the Amazon Account you want to redeem with on you're Browser
2. Visit Amazon Redeem
3. Once the WebSite has loaded, Fill in the Code and press "Apply To Your Balance";


4. Enjoy you're updated balance
